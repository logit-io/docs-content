# How do I view my Account Administrators?

### How to view my Account Administrators?

You can view the ownership details by logging into your dashboard, selecting 
'Help and Support' then clicking the 'Account Admin' option on the side menu. 
From here you can view the owner(s) and key contacts for the account.
