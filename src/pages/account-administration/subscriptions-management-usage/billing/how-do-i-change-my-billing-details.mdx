# How do I change my billing details?

### Viewing your billing details

Providing you have the correct permissions ('Owners' 'Administrators' or 'Billing' Teams) you can view your billing details by logging into your dashboard, selecting 'Account Settings' then clicking the 'Billing' option on the side menu. 

### Changing your billing details

If you require any changes to be made to your billing details or invoicing address please contact us either via our live chat or email us at [support@logit.io ](mailto:support@logit.io )
