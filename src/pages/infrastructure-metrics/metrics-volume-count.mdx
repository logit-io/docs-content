## Working with Metrics in Infrastructure Metrics

To locate your metrics usage statistics in Infrastructure Metrics, 
navigate to the Infrastructure Metrics section of your dashboard 
(see image above), below Log Management, and above Application 
Performance Monitoring. From here, select 'Statistics' in the top 
right corner of this section. This will take you to a page 
titled 'Usage Statistics'.

![Usage Statistics](@/images/help/infrastructure-metrics/full-usage-statistics.png)

Here you can view a range of statistics relating to your metrics and usage. 
You can view the number of active metrics used today and their volume. 
To find your metrics volume count, navigate to the Historic Usage, 
Metrics Count, or Usage Table section where you can view the number 
of metrics used for a given time period. For the time period, you can 
select either 14 days, 30 days, 60 days, 90 days, 180 days, or 365 days.
